☠ CURSED HAUL ☠

Compatible for PC only

You wake up alone inside a pitch-black maze. Somewhere in the darkness, 5 Diamonds are scattered across the corridors — stolen relics you must recover before the exit will open. But you are not alone. Restless Ghosts haunt every passage, and each diamond you grab draws more of them toward you. Collect all 5 diamonds and the gate unlocks — but beware: 3 Behemoths will spawn and hunt you relentlessly. Each Behemoth takes 4 bullets to kill, and the ancient ♦ AHAKA guards the gate — it takes 10 bullets to slay and cloaks everything around it in darkness. Scavenge bullet cases to arm yourself, hunt for the ⚡ Power Switch to light the maze and reveal your path, and escape before they consume you.
